Joining & Admissions

How do I join Symphony Limited?

To join us, you need to visit our official website and navigate to the 'Admissions' portal. You can start by creating an account and selecting your preferred program.

Why should I join Symphony Limited?

We offer world-class mentorship, state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, and a curriculum designed to align with industry demands. Our graduates consistently secure positions at top-tier global firms.

How can I apply?

Applications are strictly online. Fill out the application form, upload your academic transcripts, and submit the required identification documents. You will receive a confirmation email upon successful submission.

What is the entrance exam timing?

Entrance exams are conducted twice a year—in February and August. Specific dates are announced one month prior and are visible on your candidate dashboard.

Facilities & Hours

Can I access lab facilities after hours?

Yes, lab facilities are open 24/7 for registered students. However, after 8:00 PM, entry requires a valid student ID card and prior authorization from your department head for safety protocols.
